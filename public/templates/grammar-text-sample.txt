Grammar Patterns Practice

Subject + Verb + Object: Basic sentence structure
Example: I eat an apple

Question: Do/Does + Subject + Verb?: Yes/No question format
Example: Do you like pizza?

Past tense: Subject + Verb-ed: Regular past tense
Example: I walked to school

Present continuous: Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing: Action happening now
Example: She is reading a book

Future: Subject + will + Verb: Simple future tense
Example: They will arrive tomorrow
